Drax '06

The Camp for Climate Action was born in Yorkshire in 2006, outside Drax coal-fired power station. Six hundred people gathered at the UK’s biggest single source of carbon dioxide for ten days of learning and sustainable living, culminating in a day of mass action against the power station. Our aim was to kick-start a social movement to tackle climate change.

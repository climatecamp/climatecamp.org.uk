Our media - TV & Radio

At the Kingsnorth, climate campers were encouraged to 'Be The Media', documenting the workshops, infrastructure, issues and actions. Reports, photos, video and blogs have been uploaded daily to Indymedia and other sites using the solar, wind and pedal powered computer suite. Additionally we had two on site studio spaces, one for Camp Radio and another for visionOnTv with dozens of interviews taking place every day.

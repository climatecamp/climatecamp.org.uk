Never Mind the Bankers newspaper

Never Mind the Bankers is a newspaper created by a number of people involved in the Camp for Climate Action. It’s been made to bring together lots of information about the Royal Bank of Scotland and its role in making climate change much worse, and hopefully inspire you to come to the Edinburgh camp in August.

You can download a PDF of the newspaper by clicking on the image below (it's a 2.9MB file).

For copies to distribute please email london[at]climatecamp.org.uk. The paper was printed in London, so if you're ever passing through, do arrange to pick some up. Any vehicles travelling pretty much anywhere, we'd love to here from you!

Even if you can’t come in August, there are regional CfCA meetings taking place all over the country all year round. Here you’ll find like minded people coming together to hatch plans and organise in their communities, trying to make the much needed changes in the here and now rather than waiting in vain for governments and business to get round to it.

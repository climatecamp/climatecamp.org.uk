Directions to the Camp

The best way to enter the site is by walking down Gogar Station Road from the A8, and turning right along Kellerstain Farm Road. From here you will be able to walk north over a field and into the actual site - see the map above.

Don't worry about getting lost - there are obvious signs pointing you in the right direction from Gogar Station Road.

Public Transport

  • Buses: From the City Centre or Haymarket, the best bus to get is the number 48 to Gogarburn (the A8). Alternatively, you can get the "Airlink" 35 bus to the airport, or a Park and Ride bus to Ingliston P&R, and walk from there.
  • Trains: Nearest train stations are Edinburgh Park or South Gyle, approx 20-30 min walk from either

How to make a donation

The Camp for Climate Action is a collective of grassroot individuals determined to act and inspire solutions to the root causes of climate change.

If solutions to the great challenges facing us are to be found, it is our belief that they will come from ordinary people, just like you. We don't rely on grants from big NGOs, and please rest assured that we reject sponsorship from corporations that themselves are part of the problem! Instead we rely on wonderful everyday people like yourself to make up a significant proportion of the contributions we need.

We'd be really grateful for your generosity and assure you that your money will be well-spent as the Camp catalyses pro-active and effective solutions to the problems of climate chaos in the UK and internationally. Please dig deep, every bit counts.

Thanks very much!

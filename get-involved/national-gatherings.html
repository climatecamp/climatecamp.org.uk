UK Gatherings

The decision making body of the Camp's UK wide process is a series of gatherings, which have taken place once every one or two months. Each gathering has a prepared agenda put together by the local host group, with assistance from the Process Group who have overall responsibility for keeping the Camp’s organising process flowing.

Meetings

Note: We now have a system of alternating "business" meetings and social evenings. Climate Camp will still be in the Cowley Club every Tuesday but one week we will do a  formal meeting and the next we will have a social/informal working group evening with the Cowley bar open.

Next meeting

There will be a neighbourhood meeting in Brighton on Tues 4th January. It appears we have the energy to be meeting weekly at the present time. In addition we have set up a number of working groups (i.e. Action, Outreach) to work on various areas, which may (or may not) have separate meetings. If you can't make it to our main meeting but would like to be involved in one or these working groups then please get in touch.

Meeting: South Coast Meeting
Date: Tues 4th January
Time: 7:30pm
Location: Cowley Club, 12 London Road, Brighton (ring bell)

Please come along with your ideas. If you want to be involved please come to the meeting or e-mail us at southcoast[at]climatecamp.org.uk. Everyone welcome. 

Next social evening

There will be a social evening in Brighton on Tues 11th January. Please come along to meet other people concerned about climate issues and keen to do something about them. The Cowley bar will be open.

Meeting: South Coast Social Evening
Date: Tues 11th January
Time: 7:30pm
Location: Cowley Club, 12 London Road, Brighton (ring bell)

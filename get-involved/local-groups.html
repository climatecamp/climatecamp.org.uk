Local Groups

There are many local climate camp groups around the country. At the camps these "neighbourhoods" have each had their own camping space and kitchen. Many of the local groups continue taking decentralised action against climate change throughout the year, and so joining one is a great way to get involved.

If you would like further information email process [@] climatecamp.org.uk

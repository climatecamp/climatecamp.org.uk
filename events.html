In February 2011, Climate Camp will be hosting ‘A Space for Change’. In a beautiful setting somewhere in the UK (tbc) we will create a space to
reflect and re-assess climate justice activism, re-dream what a radical movement can be and re-invigorate ourselves and our network.
Don’t worry, we won’t be braving the cold in a field, we’ll be warm and cosy indoors!
It will probably be 5 days long, some time between 19 and 27 February 2011. The format is mostly to be decided there by the people that come. We
will be inviting other people and groups that we feel could contribute valuable experiences and ideas. We’ll have the option of making big
decisions about Climate Camp, but are under no pressure to do so. There’ll be space for walks, talks, creativity and partying! Watch this space for
more details.
GET INVOLVED
If you’d like to get involved in the preparations for A Space for Change, please email spaceforchange@climatecamp.org.uk.
